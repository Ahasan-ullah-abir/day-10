Important of tree

Trees play a crucial role in sustaining life on Earth. They provide oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, and improve air quality by filtering pollutants. Trees help conserve water, reduce soil erosion, and support biodiversity by offering habitat for various species. In urban areas, they provide shade, lower temperatures, and enhance mental well-being by creating green spaces. Economically, trees provide resources like timber, fruits, and medicines. By mitigating climate change, improving health, and maintaining ecological balance, trees are essential for the well-being of both humans and the planet.
